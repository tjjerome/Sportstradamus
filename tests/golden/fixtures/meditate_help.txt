Usage: meditate [OPTIONS]

  Train or retrain LightGBMLSS models for each configured market.

Options:
  --force / --no-force            Force update of all models
  --league [All|NFL|NBA|MLB|NHL|WNBA]
                                  Select league to train on
  --rebuild-filter / --no-rebuild-filter
                                  Train with full feature set (ignore Filtered),
                                  then rerun SHAP and rewrite filter
  --reset-markets TEXT            Comma-separated league:market pairs (or just
                                  market for active league) to clear from
                                  Filtered before training
  --help                          Show this message and exit.
